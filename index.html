One-armed bandit

Wallet: 100$

Rules

1.Enter the bill in the input, remember that the bill cannot be less than one and greater than your money in your wallet.

2.After entering the correct amount, press the spin button.

3.Then the draw will start. When three of the same colors appear on the board, or each of them will be different, you win, otherwise you lose.

4.When you win, your funds will be multiplied by three, and if you lose, you lose the value of your bet.
